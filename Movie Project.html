The Top 3 Movies of All Time According to Willian

My top 3 movies of all time in no specific order

Interstellar

One of the best, emotional and dramatically charged movies that the soundtrack ALONE deserves an award.
They really made a masterpiece with this movie, and the characters were fully immersed into the experience.

Spiderman: Across the Spiderverse

One of the best movies to come out of the 2020s, this movie was definitely a movie made with a comedic stance
and a strong, serious tone towards the movie. Broke a lot of traditions and created one of the best movies that
that fully immerses you, the audience into the experience, but drops a cliffhanger and then makes you want to watch its sequel.

GranTurismo: The Movie

One of the greatest movies that brings you into the world of VR racing and its impact into the real world
The acting, the documentary, the entire premise of the movie was amazing and left NO room unexplored.
